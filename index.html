Let's talk about bullying

My Story

What is bullying?

Bullying is when you keep picking on someone because you think you are smarter, cooler, stronger or better than them. Most of the time people will bully because they are jealous or because they think they better than the bullied.

Different types of bullying

Verbal - saying or writing mean things

Social - hurting someone's reputation or relationships; excluding someones

Physical - hurting a person's body or belongings

Cyber - using social media to intimidate, humiliate, stalk or/and spread rumors

What to do when being bullied

  • SPEAK UP
  • tell them to stop
  • Go talk to an adult you trust

AND ABOVE ALL DO NOT FEEL LIKE IT IS YOUR FAULT. IT IS IN NO WAY YOUR FAULT. YOU SHOULD NOT FEEL GUILTY FOR BEING YOURSELF.
